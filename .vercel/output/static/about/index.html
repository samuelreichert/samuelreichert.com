About

A frontend engineer
with a long-term thesis.

I'm Samuel — a Senior Frontend Engineer based in Amsterdam. I build for the web, love what React Native is becoming, and treat every interface as a small piece of writing.

The work

Crafting interfaces that
feel inevitable.

For over a decade I've shipped products in React, TypeScript and the half-dozen tools that orbit them. The titles changed — engineer, lead, senior — but the obsession didn't: how a screen feels when you reach for it, how typography breathes at every breakpoint, how a click can feel like a decision instead of a step.

React Native pulled me back into the mobile world. There's something honest about a phone in the hand — no clever pointer states, no excuses. Just motion, weight, and intent.

Good software, like good tailoring, hides its seams. The user shouldn't notice the work — only feel that they're in capable hands.

The person

More than a developer.

Outside the editor I travel — slowly, when I can. I read across genres (high fantasy on Sundays, Buffett's letters on Mondays). I treat investing as a long game and the gym as my daily reset. The thread that ties it all together is the same thing I look for in code: a pursuit of compounding returns.
